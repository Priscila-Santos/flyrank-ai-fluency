# What Am I Proving?

My goal is to give that hiring manager enough confidence to invite me to a technical interview.

My resume and LinkedIn describe my experience, but they cannot demonstrate how I think, solve problems, and collaborate with AI to build maintainable software. This portfolio provides concrete evidence of those abilities.
