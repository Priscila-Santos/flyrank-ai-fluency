# Identity Kit

## Brand

**Name:** Priscila Santos

**Audience:** Frontend Engineering Managers hiring AI-assisted Frontend Engineers.

**Brand Personality:**
- Creative
- Modern
- Professional
- Minimal
- Confident
- Thoughtful

The portfolio should communicate technical competence through clean execution rather than decorative visuals.

---

## Logo

Use the existing "PS – Priscila Santos" monogram as the primary logo.

The logo should always have generous whitespace around it and should never be stretched, rotated, or recolored.

Use the "PS" monogram as the favicon.

---

## Typography

### Heading Font

- Section titles

- Page headings

600–700

### Body Font

Use for:

- Navigation

- Captions

## Components

Buttons

Primary
- Background: #E11D48
- Text: White
- Rounded corners
- Slight hover elevation

Secondary
- White background
- Dark text
- Thin border

Cards

- Light border
- Soft shadow
- Rounded corners
- Comfortable spacing

Navigation

Minimal.

No decorative effects.

Clear active state.

---

## Icons

Use simple outline icons.

Recommended:
- Lucide
- Heroicons

Avoid colorful icons.

---

## Motion

Animation duration:
150–250ms

Use:
- opacity
- translateY
- scale (very subtle)

Avoid:
- bouncing
- spinning
- excessive motion

---

## Tone

The interface should feel:

Modern.

Creative.

Editorial.

Premium.

Confident.

Professional.

The design should support the projects rather than compete with them.

Prioritize clarity, accessibility, consistency, and maintainability.
